App Review Demo Access

The app now includes a visible “App Review Demo” section on the login screen.
Reviewers can access the app without regional or IP restrictions by using either:

1. One-tap review buttons on the login screen:
- Isletme Demo
- Kurye Demo
- Musteri Demo

2. Manual demo credentials:
- Admin / Isletme: isletme / isletme
- Courier / Kurye: kurye / kurye
- Customer / Musteri: musteri / musteri

All three demo accounts open with local sample data and do not require backend, regional access, or IP-based access.
This allows App Review to verify the main app flows, including:
- dashboard
- orders
- subscribers
- products
- couriers
- finance
- customer ordering
- courier delivery flow

If needed, please start with “Isletme Demo” to review the full business/admin functionality.
